☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis – Key Insights
Executive Summary

The Coffee Shop generated $698,812.33 in total sales with 149,116 customer footfalls during the analysis period. The average bill per customer was $4.69, indicating a high-volume, low-ticket business model focused on repeat purchases.

📊 Sales Performance Highlights
1. Overall Business Performance
Total Revenue: $698,812.33
Total Footfall: 149,116 customers
Average Bill Per Customer: $4.69
Average Orders per Customer: 1

Insight: The business relies heavily on customer volume rather than high-value transactions. Increasing average order value through upselling could significantly boost revenue.

⏰ Peak Sales Hours Analysis
Quantity Sold by Hour
Sales activity rises sharply between 7 AM and 10 AM.
Peak quantity occurs around 10 AM (~2,900 units).
Sales gradually decline after 11 AM.
Lowest sales are observed after 7 PM.

Business Recommendation:

Increase staffing during morning rush hours.
Launch breakfast combo offers between 7 AM–10 AM.
Promote evening discounts to improve late-day sales.

Conclusion

The analysis reveals that the coffee shop's revenue is primarily driven by Coffee and Tea products,
with strong demand during morning hours and on Sundays. The highest-performing store is Hell's Kitchen, 
while Barista Espresso is the leading product by revenue. Future growth can be achieved by increasing 
average order value, optimizing staffing during peak hours, and leveraging targeted promotions 
on lower-performing days. 🚀
